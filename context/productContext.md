# Product Context (The Why)

## Elevator Pitch
A Python-based Techno-Economic Model for Solar + BESS in Vietnam.

## Goal
Audit and replicate legacy Excel models in Python with deterministic, auditable outputs.

## Key Features
- 3-period TOU tariff structure (EVN: Peak, Standard, Off-Peak)
- Synthetic DPPA / CfD settlement (FMP vs CFMP handling)
- Battery augmentation in Year 11 & 22 funded via MRA schedule
- 25-year lifetime modeling with degradation and tax holiday logic
- Outputs: Project IRR, Equity IRR, NPV, and audit-ready reports
